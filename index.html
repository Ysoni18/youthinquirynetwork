Our Pillars

Inclusivity

Inclusivity is foundational to creating an environment where all students, regardless of their background or resources, feel welcomed, valued, and supported. We acknowledge the importance of accessibility and diversity within the STEM field and strive to ensure that every high school student has the opportunity to participate, learn, and contribute.

Innovation

Innovation is at the core of advancing the STEM fields, promoting the pursuit of new knowledge, technologies, and solutions to complex problems. Our commitment is to encourage and empower students to exercise creative thinking, problem-solving, and the exploration of uncharted territories in research.

Collaboration

Collaboration is the power of collective effort and shared knowledge in achieving greater outcomes than what could be accomplished individually hence why we aim to build an inclusive community. We promote the importance of teamwork, communication, and the exchange of ideas among students, mentors, and the broader scientific community.

What We Offer

Mentorship

Scholar Inquiry Program

This program pairs students with mentors to guide them through the entire research paper process, from writing to publication. It fosters a supportive environment where students can explore their curiosity and contribute their findings to academia.

Showcasing Mentorship

This program goes beyond publication, offering guidance on presenting research or converting it into science fair projects. Mentors help students find the best way to share their work for maximum impact.

Career Pathway

This program isn't just about publishing research. It also helps students pick a major or research area they love by guiding them through a research project. This fosters a connection between their academic interests and future careers.

Workshops

At the Youth Inquiry Network, we're committed to equipping our students with the tools, knowledge, and inspiration they need to excel in their research journey. Our carefully curated workshops are designed to do just that—by offering expert research advice and connecting you with seasoned professionals from various fields. Here's what you can expect:

Expert Research Guidance

This program offers one-on-one support throughout your research journey. They'll help you brainstorm ideas, pick the perfect approach (methodology), and analyze your findings to craft a powerful paper.

Professional Insights

This program brings research to life! You'll gain insights from industry experts and learn how research drives progress in STEM fields (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math).

Collaboration

Our Review Program is strategically divided into two key subsections to cater to diverse research needs: Peer Review and Professional Review.

Peer Review Section

This program understands the importance of students learning from each other. They might connect you with other students for peer review, which is where students get feedback on their work from other students.

Professional Review

This program pairs you with a mentor to get feedback on your research paper, but only after you've completed at least half of it. This ensures you have a solid foundation before receiving expert guidance.
